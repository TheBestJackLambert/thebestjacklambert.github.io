WATER GAME ROBOT

A floating robot with VEX motors, chain drive, and high-efficiency propellers.

VEX ROBOTICS ROBOTC MECHANICAL HYDRODYNAMICS

// 3D_MODEL

// GALLERY

// GOAL

Create a robot that floats, maneuvers in water, and pushes an opponent into a wall before being pushed into one. Build fits within a 1-foot cube; electronics are ≥1.5" above the water.

// DESIGN_HIGHLIGHTS

  • Two in-water props driven via chain from geared motor clusters
  • High gear ratio (1:48) for prop RPM
  • Lightweight frame + bottle buoyancy
  • Differential control for tight turning
  • High efficiency propeller design from MIT
  • Variable speed for each propeller including direction changes

// ITERATION_NOTES

  • Buoyancy trim with an aft bottle solved pitch issues
  • Hot-glued enclosure added sealed air volume
  • Gear cracked due to high torque
  • High tension on chain leading to gears getting stuck on axles
